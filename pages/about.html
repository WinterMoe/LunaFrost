About LunaFrost Translator

Bringing Korean web novels to English readers with AI-powered translation

About

LunaFrost Translator is a free service that specializes in translating Korean Novelpia novels using AI of your choice. Whether you prefer Claude, GPT, Gemini, or other AI models, you have the flexibility to select the one that works best for you.

This service is provided completely free of charge. However, if you'd like to support the project and help cover server costs, you can donate via PayPal using the link at the bottom of this page.

If you have any feature requests or run into any issues, please let me know via the contact form or by opening an issue on GitHub.

Open Source

LunaFrost Translator is an open-source project. We believe in transparency and community contribution. If you're interested in contributing or want to run your own instance, check out our GitHub repository.

Legal & Ethical Use

LunaFrost is designed for personal use and language learning. We respect the intellectual property rights of original authors and publishers. Users are responsible for ensuring their use of this tool complies with applicable copyright laws and the terms of service of source websites. We encourage supporting official translations and original authors whenever possible.

Privacy & Cookies

LunaFrost respects your privacy. We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and store your reading preferences locally in your browser. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or share your data with third parties. Your novels and translations are stored privately and securely.
